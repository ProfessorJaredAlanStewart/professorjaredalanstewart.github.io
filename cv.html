★ Selected Projects

The Civic Action Project (CAP)
A complete civic-engagement curriculum replacing government exams with 39 hands-on activities. Free and open, with full rubrics, tracking tools, and student-outcome data. The subject of ongoing doctoral research.
Texas In Action (TIA)
The Texas Government counterpart to CAP, connecting students to the state and local institutions that shape daily life in Texas.
Trailblazer Treks & Games Library
31 interactive, browser-based learning modules plus a library of classroom simulations and review games for American and Texas government — all free and open.
